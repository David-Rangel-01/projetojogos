Eldoria: The Corrupted Veil

A once glorious medieval kingdom has fallen from grace, corrupted by a magical plague that transforms creatures and souls. The player is a solitary hero, an exile, or a shadow knight, who must unravel the mysteries of the corruption and fight against grotesque monsters and dark lords to either restore or condemn the realm. The atmosphere is one of despair, sacrifice, and brutal combat.
